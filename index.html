Changxing Liu / 刘畅行

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Technology at Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), advised by Prof. Siheng Chen. Prior to my Ph.D., I received my Bachelor's degree in Electronic Science and Technology from SJTU and was awarded Shanghai Outstanding Graduate.

My research focuses on building generalizable embodied intelligence, especially efficient collaborative embodied agent systems for diverse and long-horizon physical-world manipulation tasks.

My current interests include Embodied Agents, Multi-modal AI, collaborative perception, autonomous driving, and vision-language-action models.
