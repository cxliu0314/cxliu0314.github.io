Changxing Liu

I am with Shanghai Jiao Tong University. My research interests include deep learning, computer vision, agentic AI, autonomous driving, embodied agents, and collaborative perception.

My recent work explores how foundation models, LLM agents, and multimodal systems can improve editable scene simulation, cooperative driving, and long-horizon embodied decision making.
